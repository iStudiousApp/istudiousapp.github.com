    How do I create a new Notebook?

    Double tap anywhere on the Desk screen and the new Notebook modal window will appear. Enter the class name and number, then tap done to save the Notebook to the desk.

    How do I add a new Text note?

    Simply double anywhere on a notebook with ONE finger and a new text box will appear. You can select the handle in the bottom right hand of the corner to resize the box.

    How do I add a new Sketch note?

    Simply double anywhere on a notebook with TWO fingers and a new text box will appear. You can select the handle in the corner of the note box and drag it out to resize the box.

    How do I resize a note box?

    Select the handle in the bottom right hand of the corner of the note box and drag it out to resize the box.

    How do I add a new Page?

    Tap the plus button on the bottom right hand corner of the notebook page and you will be presented with a new page. If you are in a section for a previous day's class, then you are presented with the option to either add a page to the current section you are on, or for the current day's section. There is no limit to the number of pages you can add!

    How do I switch between Pages in my Section?

    Simply tap the right or left arrow to move between pages.

    How do I get back to my previous notes?

    Tap on the icon on the bottom left hand of the notebook paper to see a list of all of the days that you've taken notes. Simply tap on the section that you want to go back to and you will be brought back to those notes.

    How do I add a new Flashcard?

    There are 2 ways to add flashcards.
    1) Highlight any text and choose the Flashcard option. This will automatically create a new flashcard with the selected text on the back of the flashcard. Just turn the flashcard to the front to type in what you want to appear on the front of the flashcard.

    2) Tap on the Flashcards button in the top right hand corner of the screen when you’re in a notebook. This will present the flashcard window. Tap the plus button and choose whether you want to add a new Text Flashcard or a new Sketch Flashcard. Once your new flashcard is created, double tap on the flashcard to either start typing or drawing.

    How do I delete a Text or Sketch note?

    Double tap on the note to bring it into focus. Then tap the Trash can icon in the bottom left to delete the note box.

    How do I delete a notebook?

    At this time, there is no way to delete a notebook. We are working on that feature, as well as the ability to archive old notebooks so they don't appear on the desk anymore, but you can still reference them for historical purposes.

    How can I change my font/size/color?

    At this time, you can not edit the text attributes or sketch attributes. That will be coming in our first update which will be out shortly!
