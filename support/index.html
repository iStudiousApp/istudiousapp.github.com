    What is the difference between iStudious - Note Taking w/ Integrated Flashcards and iStudious - Flashcards w/ Handwriting and Rich Text?

    iStudious - Note Taking w/ Integrated Flashcards is a combined note taking app and flashcard app designed for the iPad. It allows you to take all your notes during class, then turn those notes into flashcards. The flashcards piece has all of the functionality that iStudious - Flashcards has. In a release coming up soon, if you have both apps, you will be able to share your flashcards from your iPad to the flashcard app on the iPhone. iStudious - Flashcards w/ Handwriting & Rich Text is a stand alone flashcard app. It integrates a rich text editor with a natural feeling pen to let you fully customize your flashcards. It has an integrated quiz system that lets you practice your cards and monitor your progress.
